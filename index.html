About Me


As a graduate student pursuing a Master's degree in Modelling in Simulation at the Defense Institute of Advanced Technology, Pune, I have developed a strong foundation in Statistics, Machine Learning, and Data Analysis, making me an ideal candidate for a career in data-driven roles.

Hailing from Agra, Uttar Pradesh, I completed my Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from the Dayalbagh Educational Institute, Agra. However, upon realizing my interest and potential in mathematics and statistics, I started seeking roles that aligned with my interests, ultimately leading me to pursue a Master's degree in Modelling in Simulation (School of Computer Engineering & Mathematical Sciences).

During my academic career, I have gained proficiency in programming languages such as Python and SQL, and have also gained hands-on experience in the Indian Institute of Tropical Meteorology, Pune, where I sharpened my technical skills and understood the importance of effective communication and teamwork in data-driven decision making.

I am excited about the potential for data science to make a significant impact on businesses and society and is eager to collaborate with experienced professionals in the field to contribute to solving complex problems using data-driven approaches. I am a quick learner, analytical thinker, and possess strong work ethics.

As a constant learner, I am committed to staying at the forefront of this rapidly-evolving field and are open to learning new skills, taking on new challenges, and continuously improving my skills to become a valuable asset to any team. With my strong academic background and practical experience through internshhips and projects, I am well-equipped to excel in any data-driven role.
